Kung Pao Tofu

Description

A dish made with cubes of cooked tofu, vegetables and peanuts.

Ingredients

  • 135g tofu
  • 1/4 red bell pepper (40g)
  • 1/4 green bell pepper (40g)
  • 1/4 yellow bell pepper (40g)
  • 1/2 small onion (70g)
  • 1 bunch celery (50g)
  • 1/2 carrot (75g)
  • 1 tablespoon cassava starch
  • 1 cup water (200mL)
  • 1/4 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon peanuts
  • 3 tablespoon shoyu sauce

Steps

  1. Cut the tofu into cubes and place in a greased pan. Bake in a preheated oven at 280°C for 10 minutes, turning the cubes halfway through.
  2. Dissolve the cassava starch in 3/4 of the water, stirring well. Set aside.
  3. Heat the olive oil in a pan and add the carrots and peanuts, sautéing for 1 minute. Add the remaining water to cook the carrots better. Add the remaining vegetables and cook for 4-5 minutes. Add the tofu and drizzle with soy sauce. Add the tapioca starch and stir gently for 5 minutes. Serve immediately.

References

  • Slywitch, Eric. Alimentação sem carne. 2. ed. São Paulo, SP: Alaúde Editorial, 2015.
