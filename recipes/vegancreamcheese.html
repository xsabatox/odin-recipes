Vegan Cream Cheese

Description

A spread or topping made with tofu.

Ingredients

  • 250g tofu
  • 1 tablespoon sour cassava starch
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon table salt

Steps

  1. Place all ingredients in a food processor or blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Transfer to a saucepan and cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until combined, for about 5 minutes.

References

  • Slywitch, Eric. Alimentação sem carne. 2. ed. São Paulo, SP: Alaúde Editorial, 2015.
